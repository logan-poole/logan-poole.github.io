🔐 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 29, 2025

  • We do not track your location history. We only use live location sharing with your permission.
  • Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.
  • All communications are encrypted using industry-standard security practices.
  • You control who sees your location and for how long.
  • We store only the minimum necessary data to make the app function.

🛠️ Data Controls

You can:

  • Request deletion of your data by emailing support@pinged.nz
  • Manage permissions via your device settings at any time
  • Disable location sharing without affecting other app features

📢 Need Help or Have Concerns?

For any questions or reports related to legal use, liability, or user behavior, please contact us directly at support@pinged.nz.
