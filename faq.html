❓ How does Pinged work?

Pinged uses GPS, maps, and AR overlays to guide you to your friends in real-time.

👀 Can I hide my location?

Yes. You can toggle your visibility anytime from the app’s Settings screen.

🔐 Is my data private?

Yes. Your messages and location data are encrypted. We never share or sell your information.

📱 When is the app launching?

Pinged will launch on Android in 2025. iOS support is planned for a future release.
